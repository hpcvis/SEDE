Welcome to SEDE 2020

29th International Conference on Software Engineering and Data Engineering

Dates: October 19-21, 2020
Location: Virtual Conference Online
Note on COVID-19:
To protect the health and safety of all conference participants, the confereence will be held remotely with online presentations.
However, the conference proceedings will be published as planned.

Sponsored by the
International Society for Computers and Their Applications (ISCA)

In conjunction with
33nd International Conference on Computer Applications in Industry and Engineering (CAINE 2020)

Proceedings will be indexed in EasyChair.

The 29th SEDE Conference is interested in gathering researchers and professionals in the domains of Software Engineering and Data Engineering to present and discuss high-quality research results and outcomes in their fields. SEDE 2019 aims at facilitating cross-fertilization of ideas in Software and Data Engineering, and hence especially encourages high-quality research integrating both domains.
