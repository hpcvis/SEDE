Submission Procedures

  • Papers will be accepted only by electronic submission in PDF format. Please include author's full name(s), address, affiliation, e-mail address, telephone number and topic area.
  • More information can be found at the ISCA website.
  • All submissions must be uploaded through EasyChair by May 31, 2021.
  • The submitted manuscript should closely reflect the final paper as it will appear in the proceedings, which will be a maximum of 10 pages (+4 pages with additional page fees) in EasyChair format. Instructions for the Preparation of Manuscripts
  • After the conference, you may submit an extended version of your paper for possible publication in the ISCA journal.
